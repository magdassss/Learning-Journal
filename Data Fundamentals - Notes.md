**3. Data sizes**

- Kilobyte (KB)	1,024 Bytes
- Megabyte (MB)	1,024 Kilobytes
- Gigabyte (GB)	1,024 Megabytes
- Terabyte (TB)	1,024 Gigabytes
- Petabyte (PB)	1,024 Terabytes
- Exabyte (EB)	1,024 Petabytes
